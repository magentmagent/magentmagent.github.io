Privacy

The games on this site can be played without creating an account.

Information entered by players

When a player submits a leaderboard score, the site may send the game name, display name typed by the player, score, board size, language, game mode, filled-cell count, turn count, finish type, and submission time to the ranking server. Do not enter a real name or identifying phrase if you do not want it shown on the public leaderboard.

When a missing word is suggested, the site may send the suggested word and language context to the suggestion server. Suggestions are reviewed before they are accepted into the playable dictionary.

Analytics and game events

The site may use Cloudflare Web Analytics to understand aggregate visits and performance. The game may also send aggregate gameplay events such as game start, finish type, board size, language, mode, and sharing action counts. These events are used to improve the game and do not include the full words typed on the board.

Advertising

If Google AdSense is enabled, Google and its partners may use cookies or similar technologies to serve, measure, and personalize ads according to Google's advertising policies and user settings. Advertising is not required to play the game.

Hosting and service providers

The static site is hosted on GitHub Pages. Ranking, suggestion, and analytics-related endpoints may be served through Cloudflare services. These providers may process technical information such as IP address, browser details, request logs, and security signals as part of hosting and abuse prevention.

Contact

For privacy questions, ranking removal requests, or concerns about a submitted display name, use the contact page and include enough detail to identify the issue.
