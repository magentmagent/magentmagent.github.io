Crown Chain

Capture pieces, transform into them, and keep the chain alive.

Best0

Basic keeps standard pieces readable. Chaos mixes in variants. Time Attack ends your turn after 3 seconds.

Rules

  • CaptureTap a piece once to preview movement. Tap a highlighted target again to capture it.

  • TransformAfter a capture, you usually become that piece and use its movement next.

  • Free MoveOnce between captures or turns, you may move to an empty square instead of capturing.

  • One HitEnd turn to spawn pieces. If an enemy can attack immediately, the run ends.
