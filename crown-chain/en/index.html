    Rules

    • Tap a piece once to preview its movement. If it can be captured, tap it again to capture.
    • After a capture, you usually become that piece and use its movement.
    • Once between captures or turns, you may move to an empty square instead of capturing.
    • Pawns are bonus pieces: capturing one gives points but does not transform you.
    • Each capture increases combo. Score is piece value times combo.
    • End turn to spawn new pieces. If no capture exists, ending turn costs 1 life.
    • Basic uses standard pieces. Chaos adds variant pieces as levels rise.
