Games

Three browser games are currently available: Crown Chain, Tower Cut, and word chain snake.

Crown Chain

A compact chess-chain puzzle where every capture transforms your current piece.

Play Basic mode with standard chess pieces or Chaos mode with variant pieces, including Assassin and Big Rook.

Basic and Chaos are ranked separately, and each mode keeps its own best-score record.

Play now

Tower Cut

A quick stack-editing puzzle where you cut broken towers and rebuild them to match the target.

Blocks can only be placed on top of a column, and cutting one block removes everything above it.

Score comes from speed, efficient clicks, PERFECT clears, and combo streaks.

Play now

word chain snake

A word-chain puzzle where each submitted word becomes a path on the board. The next word starts from the current head, crossings are allowed only when letters match, and repeated words are blocked.

The game currently supports English, Korean, and Japanese dictionaries, board-size leaderboards, a classic mode, and a snake mode with bonus letters placed on the board.

Word lists are filtered for playability, and missing words can be suggested in the game for review.

Play now
