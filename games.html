Games

This site is being built as a small collection of browser games, starting with word chain snake.

word chain snake

A word-chain puzzle where each submitted word becomes a path on the board. The next word starts from the current head, crossings are allowed only when letters match, and repeated words are blocked.

The game currently supports English, Korean, and Japanese dictionaries, board-size leaderboards, a classic mode, and a snake mode with bonus letters placed on the board.

Word lists are filtered for playability, and missing words can be suggested in the game for review.
