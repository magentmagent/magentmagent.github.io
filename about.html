About

magentmagent games is a small browser-game site operated as a personal web project.

The site provides free games that can be played without creating an account: Word Chain Snake, Crown Chain, and Tower Cut.

The games are designed for short sessions on mobile and desktop, with rankings, sharing, and feedback flows where they fit the game.

Game scores, word suggestions, and aggregate analytics may be used to operate and improve the site. User feedback is handled through the public project issue tracker.
